## ğŸ“– Usage

### Generating Titles

1. **Select a Video**: Choose a video from your photo library
2. **Choose Platforms**: Select one or more platforms (YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, TikTok)
3. **Select Language**: Choose your preferred language for the titles
4. **Generate**: Tap the generate button (requires coins)
5. **Review**: View and copy your generated titles

### Earning Coins

- **Watch Ads**: Earn 10 coins by watching a rewarded advertisement
- **Purchase Packages**: Buy coin packages through in-app purchases
- **Initial Bonus**: Receive 40 coins when you first launch the app

### Managing Titles

- **View History**: Browse all your previously generated titles
- **Delete Titles**: Remove individual titles or clear all titles
- **Copy Titles**: Easily copy any generated title to use in your social media posts
